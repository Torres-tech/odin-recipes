Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  3. Cook ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly.
  6. Let cool and serve.
