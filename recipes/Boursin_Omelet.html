Boursin Omelet

Home

Description

This Boursin omelet is a quick, luxurious breakfast that brings creamy texture and rich flavor to the classic omelet. Finish with a sprinkle of chives for a fresh pop of color.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh chives
  • 1 ounce soft French cheese, such as Boursin cheese (any flavor), crumbled

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  3. Cook ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly.
  6. Let cool and serve.
